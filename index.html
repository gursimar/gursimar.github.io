About

I'm a graduate student in the Computer Science Department at the University of British Columbia (UBC). I'm supervised by Jim Little and is interested in applications of deep learning related to reasoning in multi-model data including videos, images and, text. Previous to this, as a research engineer at Aspiring Minds Research labs, India, I worked on automatic grading of computer programs, in terms of their functional correctness, stylistics, and runtime efficiency (link). I'm a recipient of the prestigious KVPY fellowship (mentored), awarded by the Government of India to encourage talented students in research.
