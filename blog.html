Research Interests

Broadly, I'm interested in machine learning and computer programming education. I'm excited in applications of machine learning techniques to scale computer programming education and in assisstive technologies to help programmers become productive and write better code. Previously, at Aspiring Minds, I worked on automatic grading of computer programs, in terms of their functional correctness, stylistics, and runtime efficiency. Earlier I have been working on embedded systems and robotics and have a couple of popular national competitions.
